How it works

Three simple habits — and the whole feeding story is at hand.

1

Log it

A feeding, a "how it went" rating, a reaction, a note and photos — in a couple of taps.

2

Watch it

Every new food goes through your plan's observation window — "day 2 of 3" right on the food card.

3

Keep it up

Pudding gently reminds you to repeat introduced allergens so tolerance isn't lost.

FAQ

Is it free?

Yes, Pudding is free at launch.

Do I need internet or an account?

No. The app works offline, no sign-up — all data stays on your device.

Does Pudding replace a pediatrician?

No. It's a diary companion: it records and reminds. Agree on timing and decisions with your doctor.

Where do the timings come from?

They're not imposed: you set the starting age, observation windows and allergen frequency yourself in "your plan" — following your pediatrician's guidance.

Is there an export for the doctor?

Yes. A PDF with the feeding history, reactions and allergen status in a couple of taps — plus a short "do not give" sheet for the nanny or daycare.
