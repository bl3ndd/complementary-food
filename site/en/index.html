Starting solids is stressful. We've got you.

When to start? What first? What about allergies? Pudding takes the guesswork out — calmly, step by step, no spreadsheets and no 3 a.m. panic.

How it works

Three simple steps and solids are under control.

1

Pick a food

Open the catalog and choose what to introduce — Pudding suggests the right age.

2

Watch the window

Log feedings, follow the "Day X of N" reaction window, and note how it went.

3

Keep allergens up

Pudding reminds you to repeat introduced allergens on time — tolerance stays strong.

Four pillars

Food catalog

Veggies, grains, fruit, meat, allergens — with clear illustrations and the age each food can be introduced.

One at a time

A new food starts an observation window. See "Day X of N" so you can calmly watch for reactions.

Allergens handled

We remind you to keep offering introduced allergens on schedule to maintain tolerance.

Calendar & reminders

A day-by-day history and gentle weekly reminders — nothing slips through.

Your data stays on your phone

No account, no ads, no tracking. Your baby's name, foods, and reactions are stored locally and never leave the device.

Grounded in pediatric guidance

Timelines in Pudding are based on WHO, AAP/NIAID, and Russian Pediatric Society guidance. Where a value is clinical consensus, we say so honestly. It's a diary, not medical advice — always make decisions together with your pediatrician.

FAQ

Is it free?

Yes, Pudding is free at launch.

Do I need internet or an account?

No. The app works offline and needs no sign-up — all data stays on your device.

Does Pudding replace a pediatrician?

No. It's a helper diary. Always confirm food and allergen timing with your doctor.

Where do the timelines come from?

From WHO, AAP/NIAID, and Russian Pediatric Society guidance. Sources are shown right in the app.

From what age?

Pick a methodology (WHO, AAP, RU) — solids usually start around 5–6 months.
